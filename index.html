Hi, my name is Clive.

I’m a UX Designer, ← Big word alert! — It simply means I spend most of my time designing experiences that help make the lives of human beings a little easier.

Here are some projects I’ve worked on:

KongaEZ

A Progressive Web App designed to provide Konga's mobile customers the benefits of its shopping app including performance, the ability to work offline, and re-engagement without the data cost.

GoMyWay

A platform that connects anyone with drivers who have spare seats to share. Travelers can easily share a safe pocket-friendly ride with drivers going their way.

Paper

A platform that helps Nigerian businesses send money to anyone and accept bank & card payments from their customers quickly, on web and on mobile.

SellerHQ

A Konga service that allows merchants sell their products and services to over 50 millions customers on Konga.com, Nigeria's Largest Online Mall.

500Shops

A platform that allows any business owner setup a complete online store in minutes. It also provides tools to easily manage inventory, receive & process customer orders and accept payments.

nPower

A programme designed by the Nigerian Government to drastically reduce youth unemployment. The focus is to provide young citizens with the skills to enable them advance to entrepreneurship.
